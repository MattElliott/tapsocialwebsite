OUR TEAM

Paul Humpherson is an independent criminal barrister, a government lawyer and a university tutor. He has done voluntary work as an advocate for domestic violence victims and helped administer restorative justice conferences with the Oxfordshire YOT.

Amy Taylor is currently undertaking doctoral research in criminology at University of Oxford. She has previous experience working for the court service, as an auxiliary police constable, in the voluntary sector with ex-offenders, in law reform and most recently in criminal justice policy in central government.

Tess Taylor is a bachelor of commerce graduate who worked for several years at an organisation helping ex-offenders to secure pardons for criminal records. She has a range of experience in different areas of the hospitality industry, most recently as general manager of a fast growing cafe specialising in craft beer.

Jason Bolger is an award winning craft brewer who is passionate about all things beer. His education started in his kitchen as a homebrewer, later training at the Siebel Institute of Technology in Chicago and continues at the Institute of Brewing and Distilling in London. What started as a hobby quickly led to a full time career in brewing, beginning at Iron Hill Brewery and Restaurant in Phoenixville, USA, and then moving to Northville Brewery and Winery in Northville, USA. Most recently (since arriving in the UK) he has been head brewer at Brewhouse and Kitchen in Portsmouth where he has developed a passion for teaching.

Matt Elliott is an economics and management graduate with experience working across a broad range of industries and sectors. He worked for several years for a large multinational consultancy firm as an economist and modeller, and having subsequently developed an enthusiasm for software coding, has written bespoke pieces of software for small businesses. Along the way he has also trained and qualified as an outdoor instructor, and taught students in maths and economics at GSCE and A level.
