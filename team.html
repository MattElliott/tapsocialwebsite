OUR TEAM

Paul Humpherson is an independent criminal barrister, a government lawyer and a university tutor. He has done voluntary work as an advocate for domestic violence victims and helped administer restorative justice conferences with the Oxfordshire YOT.

Amy Taylor is a criminologist who has a range of experience across the criminal justice system, having worked with the police, the court service, in the voluntary sector with ex-offenders, in law reform and in criminal justice policy in central government. She will shortly be commencing a PhD in criminology at University of Oxford.

Tess Taylor is a bachelor of commerce graduate who worked for several years at an organisation helping ex-offenders to secure pardons for criminal records. She has a range of experience in different areas of the hospitality industry and currently manages a fast growing bar specialising in craft beer.
