ABOUT US

Our ambition is to lead a social movement!

Using the craft beer revolution we are aiming to unlock opportunities for marginalised communities, including for people that have had contact with the criminal justice system. By building a sustainable brewing business, we will provide employment and training opportunities for people who are trying to turn their lives around. And by creating strong links with surrounding businesses, services and neighbourhoods we will provide a safe space through which community relationships can flourish.

While it may not be the most obvious combination, we believe that the craft beer industry represents a really exciting opportunity to engage people who have had a hard time in the past. Brewing is fun. It is aspirational and interesting, it requires skill, and there is a rapidly growing market for it. And importantly, the pub represents a cornerstone of British culture and social life.

With a range of experience across the criminal justice sector and hospitality and brewing industries, We are a diverse team of passionate, engaged, hard-working individuals who are well-placed to be able to have a really huge social impact. But as with all community movements, we recognise that this one needs strong leadership coupled with real life experience and insight. While we share a strong vision, we want to make sure that we are trusted and well connected to the community. In addition, we want to make sure that we develop and stick to a core strategy for achieving our vision of driving social change through craft beer. To this end, we have a team of experienced advisors from across the local community and the criminal justice system who guide our development.
